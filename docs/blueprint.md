# **App Name**: WinWin Tint Landing

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with CTA: Prominent hero section featuring a high-quality image, bold headline ('Enhance. Protect. WinWin.'), and a 'Get a Free Quote' button that scrolls to the contact form.
- About Section: Briefly introduce WinWin Window Tint, highlighting protection, privacy, and perfection.
- Service Highlights: Showcase Automotive Tinting, Home/Office Tinting, and Ceramic Film Options in a 3-column layout.
- Before & After Gallery: An image gallery displaying before and after examples of window tinting projects.
- Contact/Quote Form: Form to capture Name, Email, Phone, Vehicle/Service Type, and Message. Data will be saved to Firestore.
- Data Submission: Upon submission of the Contact Form, capture the submitted information and write the record into the Firestore database.
- Footer Section: Include business hours, service area (San Antonio, TX), and links to Instagram and Google Reviews in the footer.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#FFD700) to convey luxury and quality.
- Background color: Dark gray (#333333) to provide a sleek, modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Light gray (#CCCCCC) for subtle highlights and visual separation.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif, for a modern and clean look.
- Use a clean, minimalist layout with plenty of white space to create a professional and uncluttered feel. Inspired by Squarespace and Tesla's websites.
- Implement smooth scroll animations when navigating between sections to enhance the user experience.
- Use simple, elegant icons to represent different services and highlight key features. Icons should complement the gold and gray color scheme.
